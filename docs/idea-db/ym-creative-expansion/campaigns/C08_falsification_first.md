# C08 — Falsification-first campaign

Goal: every attractive formula must survive tiny finite models before it becomes a construction task.

Tests:
- empty family;
- singleton family;
- two different indices with same target;
- non-injective source-bond map;
- zero scale or zero denominator;
- wrong sign exponent;
- metric inequality reversed;
- positivity missing on amplitude.

Deliverable:
- `falsification_results/<card_id>.json`
- status: `killed`, `needs_assumption`, `toy_survives`, or `ready_for_lean_stub`.
